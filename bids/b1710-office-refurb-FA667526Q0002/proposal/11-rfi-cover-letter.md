Blue Print Constructs (RK Residential Homes and Commercial Constructions, LLC dba Blue Print Constructs, UEI **LM4YHVQ71QG7**, CAGE **9LET0**) is preparing an offer in response to Solicitation FA667526Q0002 (B1710 Office Refurbishment at NAS JRB Fort Worth), due 29 May 2026 at 17:00 CDT.

In support of preparing a responsive and accurately-priced offer, we request clarification on the following items. We have used the attached `Request For Information Form.pdf` (Attachment 05 of the RFQ) as the format basis.

1. **Site walk coordination.** Section "SITE VISITATION" of the SOW states *"Prior to submitting proposals, potential contractors shall walk the facility to confirm all spaces and measurements are accurate and understood."* No site-walk date, time, or POC for base-access sponsorship is listed in the RFQ package. Is the Contracting Office offering a coordinated site walk in advance of 29 May 17:00? If yes, please provide the date/time and the base-access sponsor POC. If no, please confirm whether offerors are expected to price based on the SOW-stated quantities (3,500 SF) with FAR 52.236-2 (Differing Site Conditions) as the post-award remedy for material variance.

2. **Primary submission email.** Section G p.10 lists `lydia.carlton@us.af.mil` and `todd.benner@us.af.mil` as the WAWF-question POCs. Please confirm `lydia.carlton@us.af.mil` is the correct email address for submission of the completed offer packet on or before 29 May 17:00 CDT.

3. **Paint color — exact match required?** SOW Item C specifies the paint color as *"Agreeable Gray"*. This is the common name for Sherwin-Williams SW 7029. Please confirm whether the Contracting Office requires SW 7029 specifically, or whether any brand color-matched to SW 7029 (e.g., Benjamin Moore, Behr, PPG) is acceptable as an or-equal.

4. **Carpet tile — Shaw at spec vs or-equal.** SOW Item B specifies Shaw **Constellat EW24**, Style 59326, Color 26110 Moonlight Grade A, **Dye Lot H6958**. The SOW also says "Shaw or other accepted contractor submission." Two clarifications: (a) is the dye lot H6958 a hard requirement (e.g., matching an existing carryover stock the Government holds), or is any current production lot of color 26110 Moonlight Grade A acceptable? (b) confirm "or accepted contractor submission" permits an or-equal substitution (Mohawk SmartStrand, Interface 24×24, etc.) subject to AF IMT 3000 approval.

5. **Acceptance period.** FAR 52.212-1 default acceptance period for commercial-item solicitations is 30 calendar days from offer due date. SF 1449 Block 13d is not pre-filled with an acceptance-period value. Please confirm 30 calendar days is the minimum acceptance period for this RFQ.

6. **AF IMT 3000 — pre-procurement vs post-procurement.** SOW §SCHEDULE OF SUBMITTALS lists "Carpet and Cove Base samples" and "Paint color code options" for government selection. Please confirm whether the AF IMT 3000 form is submitted (a) post-NTP for material approval before procurement, or (b) with the offer as part of the technical proposal.

Thank you for your time. We respect that responses to RFIs typically take 2-3 business days to issue and we are submitting this RFI on 28 May 2026 with the offer deadline of 29 May at 17:00 in mind. Any answers we receive between submission and offer-close will inform our final offer or a SF 30 amendment acknowledgement as appropriate.
