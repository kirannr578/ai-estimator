## 4. Why this matters for the offer

- **Section M considers technical understanding.** A clean Davis-Bacon affirmative in the offer demonstrates understanding of the regulatory regime and reduces CO concern about a small-business offeror underpricing labor.
- **A cost build-up that ignores DBA rates is a red flag.** Pricing labor at non-prevailing rates would either (a) underprice the bid (CO sees as irresponsible) or (b) lead to post-award certified-payroll non-compliance + funds withholding.
- **Davis-Bacon labor uplift is already baked into the price proposal** per `../10-prevailing-wages.md` § G and `../09-price-references.md`.

## 5. Subcontracting flow-down

If BPC subs out any portion of the work (e.g., flooring install per the strategic option in `../04-evaluation-strategy.md` §6), the sub must:
- Pay DBA-compliant rates
- Submit weekly WH-347 certified payroll (prime collects and submits with prime's payroll)
- Sign WH-348 Statement of Compliance
- Maintain records for 3 years post-completion

The prime contractor is **responsible for sub compliance** per FAR 52.222-11. Sub progress payments are conditioned on receipt of certified payroll.

## 6. Common Davis-Bacon pitfalls (to avoid)

| Pitfall | Avoidance |
|---|---|
| Paying carpet installer the "Laborer" rate instead of "Floor Layer" rate | Use exact WD trade titles; default to the higher rate if classification is ambiguous |
| Misclassifying journeyman as apprentice to save cost | Track apprentice-to-journeyman ratio per registered apprenticeship program; fines for misclassification are 1.5× back wages + civil penalties |
| Rolling fringe into base rate without disclosure on WH-347 | Show fringe as separate line item: either cash-paid or paid-into-bona-fide-plan |
| Submitting WH-347 1+ weeks late | Submit every Monday for the prior week; even 1-week delay triggers CO funds withholding under 52.222-8 |
| Sub neglecting to submit WH-347 | Prime holds sub progress payment until WH-347 received |
| Using a non-DOL apprenticeship program | DOL or State Apprenticeship Agency-registered programs only per FAR 52.222-9 |
| Not posting the WD at the jobsite | Print + display the Attachment 03 PDF in a visible site office location |
| Working employees over 40 hrs without overtime per Contract Work Hours and Safety Standards Act | CWHSSA requires 1.5× for hours over 40/week; some federal CO's add this to flow-down |
