## Alternative — Irrevocable Letter of Credit (ILC)

If the surety route is not available, BPC may submit an ILC from the firm's banking relationship. The ILC route is structurally equivalent under FAR 52.228-13:

- 100% of contract price
- Valid for performance period plus 1 year
- Irrevocable (cannot be modified or canceled without CO consent)
- Issued by a bank acceptable to the U.S. Government (typically the firm's bank-of-record)
