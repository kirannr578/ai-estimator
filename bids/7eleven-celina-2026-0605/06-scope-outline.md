Bundle these into a single sub package quote to Ready Construction. Estimated package magnitude: **$80K – $250K** depending on actual quantities and finish system specs.

## What BPC explicitly will NOT quote

- **Fueling system (UST + dispensers + lines)** — TCEQ-regulated specialty BPC does not hold the license for
- **Brand-specific specialty installs (Mapes canopy, Jimco canopy, Royston cooler trim if not OFCI)** — defer to specialty subs
- **Major civil scopes (Excavation, Site Utilities, Concrete Paving, Concrete Foundations)** — outside BPC's natural lane on a c-store pad and do not fit the 6-day fuse for sub outreach

## Critical questions for Ready Construction

1. Total project magnitude / pad cost?
2. Is Ready Construction looking for narrow single-trade subs or broader rolled-up packages?
3. OFCI / CFCI matrix for Royston millwork, cooler/refrigeration equipment, beverage equipment
4. Schedule — NTP, slab pour, dry-in target, substantial completion, store-open target
5. Liquidated damages?
6. Bond requirement at sub tier?
7. Insurance limits / additional-insured wording (CGL, auto, WC, umbrella)
8. Whether MBE participation is favorable in Ready Construction's owner reporting
