  RFI #1: GFE aluminum ramp specifications (Option 001)

    Question: Please provide manufacturer cut-sheet, length, width, weight,

  RFI #2: ADA slope compliance scope confirmation (Option 001)
    Reference: SOW Att 1 §10 (Option 001)
    Question: Please confirm whether the ramp extension must meet ADA-AS
              accessibility standards (1:12 max slope, 36 in clear width,

    Reason: ADA compliance affects landing area sizing and intermediate-rest

    Reference: SOW Att 1 §11 (Option 002)
    Question: SOW specifies enclosure on three sides of the cabin perimeter.
              Please confirm WHICH three sides (suggest excluding the side
              facing the prevailing storm direction or the side housing the
              ramp). Site visit observation will inform but written direction
              is preferred.
    Reason: Layout affects breakaway-design intent and perimeter LF takeoff.

  RFI #4: Door master-keying directive (CLIN 0001)
    Reference: SOW Att 1 §6
    Question: Please confirm whether the three new keyed deadbolts must be
              master-keyed to a NPS standard (or to which key system) or
              whether independent keying is acceptable.
    Reason: Master-keying adds locksmith / cylinder-cutting cost.

  RFI #5: Roof damage extent and roof scope cap (CLIN 0002)
    Reference: SOW Att 1 §7
    Question: Please indicate (a) approximate sq-ft of shingle replacement
              expected, (b) whether the lump sum is intended to include
              underlayment / decking repair if discovered during demo, or
              (c) whether a maximum sq-ft cap applies to the lump sum.
    Reason: As a lump-sum CLIN, scope-cap clarity prevents both
            under-pricing and exception-language risk in the proposal.

  RFI #6: NPS Preservation Brief 4 applicability (CLIN 0002)
    Reference: SOW Att 1 §7
    Question: Is the cabin a contributing or non-contributing resource on
              the National Register of Historic Places? Does NPS Preservation
              Brief 4 (Roofing for Historic Buildings) apply to the
              like-kind shingle replacement?
    Reason: Brief 4 dictates fastener type, exposure, and venting and may
            require a HABS / HSR consultation step pre-install.

  RFI #7: Period of Performance — official start

              ("All on-site work must be completed before 01 JUNE 2025") vs.
              Section B / Special Notice ("60 calendar days from NTP")
    Question: SF-18 and SOW state stale dates from a prior solicitation.
              Please confirm the operative POP is 60 calendar days from NTP,
              and confirm the latest acceptable NTP date.
    Reason: Three different POP statements in the package; we are pricing
            against "60 cal-days from NTP."

  RFI #8: Wildlife-buffer / nesting-season restrictions
    Reference: PAIS general operating policy (not stated in SOW)
    Question: Do Kemp's Ridley sea-turtle nesting buffers (Apr–Jul typically
