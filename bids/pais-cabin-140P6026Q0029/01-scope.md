## 1. Project summary

The primary work of this project includes, but is not limited to, **the procurement, repair, and improvement of physical security and weather protection** at a single coastal-wood-framed cabin near a saltwater environment in Padre Island National Seashore. Work includes door reinforcement (CLIN 001), roof leak repair with marine-grade materials (CLIN 002), installation of ten (10) CAT5 TDI hurricane-rated Bahama shutters (CLIN 003), and two priced options for a code-compliant ramp extension (Option 001) and a breakaway sand-control enclosure on three sides of the raised cabin (Option 002). Cabin location is approximately 50 miles south of Hwy 361 on Park Road 22; backcountry access requires a 4-wheel-drive vehicle.
