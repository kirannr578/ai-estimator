## What the proposal MUST include (best-value SAP — not LPTA)

Unlike a pure LPTA proposal, the technical-capability narrative is **scored**, not pass/fail. Per Section M:

- **Price** — comparative cost evaluation; CO may request cost breakdown
- **Technical capability** — schedule + approach + documentation that BPC has the technical knowledge, capability, business and managerial acumen, and experience to perform; relevant photos, awards, certifications welcome
- **Prior experience** — 3 to 5 references, similar size + scope, last 5 years, with verifiable POCs

> Discipline reminder for SAP best-value: keep the narrative **focused** (Park Service evaluators read fast; this is SAP not full Part 15) but **include enough detail that the evaluation team can discern thorough understanding**. Section L explicitly warns that insufficient detail "may lead to this item being deemed insufficient." Don't go silent on approach.

## What the proposal must NOT include

- ❌ Alternate stipulations to the requirements ("Quotes offering alternative stipulations to the requirements of this solicitation will NOT be considered or accepted") — no exception language
- ❌ Alternate pricing or alternate quotes
- ❌ Foreign-construction-material reliance without a Buy American FAR 52.225-9(b)(3) request
- ❌ Caveats, conditions, or qualifications appended to the SOW
