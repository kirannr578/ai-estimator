## ⚠️ Headline gating risks

This RFP is materially larger than BPC's recent bid history. **A go/no-go review must precede any further investment.** Specifically:

1. **Magnitude is 5–10× BPC's recent range.** Recent BPC bids (B1710 Office Refurb, CHS Cafeteria, Angelo State Carr EFA) have all been under $1M. This target price range is $5M–$10M.
2. **Bonding capacity.** $5M–$10M projects require bid bond (FAR 52.228-1, 20% of bid or $1M cap) + 100% performance + 100% payment bonds (FAR 52.228-15) post-award. BPC's current single-project surety floor is $1M per `firm/firm-profile.json` — needs explicit surety conversation before bid.
3. **Internal capacity.** Design-bid-build construction of a staging facility with an operations building, container loading aprons, security fencing, tree-replacement options, and full MEP scope (Divisions 22, 23, 25, 26, 27) is at least a 12–18 month POP requiring multiple supervised crews. BPC's current PM bench needs explicit confirmation it can run a project of this size in parallel with other commitments.
4. **Bonding capacity for the bid itself.** FAR 52.228-1 bid bond is "20% of bid OR $1M whichever less." For a $5M-$10M bid, that's a $1M bid bond (cap). BPC's current bond letter floor must be confirmed at this level.
5. **Past performance.** Federal Part-15 tradeoff source-selection weights past performance heavily. BPC's past-performance portfolio (Hindu Temple ~$350K, Lavon RV Park ~$500K, Holiday Inn ~$300K, plus 250+ SFH) does not include a federal-construction prior at $5M+. This is a relevance / similarity gap.
6. **Spec book scale.** The compiled RFP is 1,835 pages spanning Divisions 00 through 33. The estimating effort is 200+ hours just for the takeoff and pricing build, before any narrative volumes.

## Why this opportunity still merits a scaffold

Despite the gating risks, this is the **first BPC exemplar of a Federal RFP under FAR Part 15 tradeoff** — the playbook for it is already written but had no real-world example. Even if BPC ultimately no-gos this bid, the scaffold itself (capturing the W9126G USACE-FWD posting structure, the spec-book TOC, the CLIN schedule with base + 5 options, the bonding posture, the SBA Case Number reference) is valuable system learning that future BPC bids in the federal-RFP-tradeoff lane will benefit from.
