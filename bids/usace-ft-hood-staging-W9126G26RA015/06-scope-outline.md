## Trade-scope summary

A staging / marshalling area at this scale typically breaks down as:

| Major scope | Primary spec divisions | BPC self vs. sub |
|---|---|---|
| Site civil (earthwork, paving, fence, stormwater, OSP) | 31, 32, 33 | Sub (civil GC) |
| Operations building shell (concrete, steel, framing, roof, windows, doors) | 03, 05, 06, 07, 08 | Sub (steel + envelope subs) |
| Interior finishes (gypsum, tile, ceilings, flooring, paint, specialties, furnishings) | 09, 10, 12 | Sub or self |
| MEP (plumbing, HVAC with BACnet, electrical, lightning protection, comms) | 22, 23, 25, 26, 27 | Multiple subs |
| FRCS cybersecurity (Section 25 05 11.01 / .02) | 25 | Specialty sub (rare; lead time) |
| Fall protection (Section 11 18 29) | 11 | Specialty sub |
| AT/OPSEC compliance (Section 01 01 10.00 44) | 01 | BPC + cleared personnel |
| QC + safety + RMS-CM | 01 | BPC self |
