## A. What this form is

A TAMU System Competitive Sealed Proposal under Tex. Gov't Code Ch. 2269 is anchored on a "Proposal Form" (also called the "Bid Form" on some packages). It is the legally-binding cover page of the priced proposal. The TAMU CSP Proposal Form typically:

- Identifies the solicitation, the project, and the proposer
- States the total lump-sum price
- States unit prices (if requested) and alternates (if requested)
- States proposed substantial completion duration
- Acknowledges all addenda
- Certifies HSP commitment
- Bears a wet-ink signature by an authorized officer

**The Angelo State Carr EFA solicitation in our inbox** (`inbox/.../ESBD_516718_..._26-007 Carr EFA Dressing Room Renocation Attachment A.pdf`) is a content-shape proxy. **The Texas TFC Bid Form structure is broadly common across Texas state higher-ed CSPs.** We expect the TAMU form to track the same structure but with:

- TAMU System / SSC Services for Education branding header
- TAMU-specific routing address (1371 TAMU / 600 Agronomy Road, Suite 218)
- TAMU System-specific allowance + alternate categories (if any)
- TAMU System-specific certification language (HUB commitment, conflict of interest, lobbying)

## C. Common form-fill failures (TAMU clerks reject for these)

1. **Legal-name mismatch.** Name on CSP Proposal Form ≠ name on W-9 ≠ name on CIQ ≠ name on 1295. Triple-check.
2. **Unsigned form.** Or photocopied signature. TAMU System pattern requires wet ink, originals only.
3. **Math errors.** Lump sum + alternates ≠ Total Proposed Contract Sum. Subtotal + unit-price multiplier ≠ stated total. Run the math twice.
4. **Missing addenda acknowledgment.** Even if no addenda were issued, the form typically has a "no addenda received" checkbox; do not leave blank.
5. **Wrong bond form.** TAMU System has its own bond forms; AIA A310 / A312 stand-ins may not be accepted at submission.
6. **Out-of-date insurance ACORDs.** ACORDs typically expire 30 days from issue for proposal use; re-pull within 30 days of submission.
7. **Missing HSP.** HSP must be a separate submitted document; not in lieu of the Proposal Form HSP-certification box, both are required.
8. **Late submission.** TAMU's clock is the wall clock at the SSC Service Solutions desk; arrive early. Late = non-responsive, no exceptions.
