## G. If the actual CSP package overrides any item above

The CSP package is authoritative. Re-author this checklist section-by-section on G2 close. In particular, watch for:

- Different number of copies (some TAMU CSPs require 1 original + 5 copies)
- Different tab order or section requirements (some require a specific cover-page format)
- Different sealed-envelope labeling rules
- Different signature requirements (wet ink vs digital)
- Different submission window rules (some allow electronic submission via the e-Builder portal as a primary or backup; verify)
- Different bond form requirements (TAMU System has specific forms that override AIA stand-ins)
