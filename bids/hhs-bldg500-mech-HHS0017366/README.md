## Headline risk

⚠️ **On-site visit window (May 28-29, 2026) already past as of scaffold creation (2026-05-30).** Attendance was "strongly encouraged but not required" per §6.8.3 of the IFB. Bidders who attended will have a meaningful information advantage — visualizing the existing metal roof condition, the industrial generator footprint, the overhead-door opening, and the interior wall surface area for paint quantification. BPC can request a make-up site visit through the HUB Coordinator exception (§6.2) or accept the disadvantage and rely on photo/satellite imagery. ## Why this opportunity

1. **Scope fit (moderate).** The work is a hybrid: light demolition + structural reinforcement + crane-rigged generator removal (hazmat-adjacent fluids) + overhead-door install + interior paint. BPC self-performs the demo, paint, and door install; the generator decommissioning and crane rigging need a specialty hazmat / rigging sub.
2. **Magnitude.** Not stated. NIGP 910-00 (Building Maintenance / Installation / Repair Services) typically ranges $25K–$250K for HHSC IFBs. The interior paint quantity (~198 sf) is small; the structural/door/generator work drives the bulk. Estimate range: **$75K – $200K**.
3. **Process fit.** HHSC IFB submission is via the HHS Online Bid Room (Exhibit E) — a multi-step IAMOnline registration BPC has not yet completed. Registration takes "several days" per Exhibit E; **start today**.
4. **POP.** "Anticipated Contract Start Date: Date of award" — no fixed POP in days. Pull from the IFB scope section once read in detail (current scaffold only has pages 1–12 extracted).
