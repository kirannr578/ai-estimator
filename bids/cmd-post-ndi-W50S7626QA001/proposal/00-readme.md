## "What not to include" (compliance traps)

- **Cost breakdown** — keep the bid FFP per CLIN. Do not enclose internal cost build-up unless the CO requests in negotiations.
- **Marketing brochures, capability statements** — not asked for; resist temptation. The Subfactor 1 list, Subfactor 2 schedule, and past-performance volume are the persuasion vehicles.
- **Long technical narratives** — Section M evaluates 2 specific subfactors, not a generic technical narrative. Don't pad.
- **Page limits not specified** — but discipline yourself to ~10–15 pages total submitted (excluding SF 1442 + bid bond + schedule). Voluminous offers signal a firm that hasn't internalized the comparative-evaluation framework.
- **Conditions or qualifications on the offer** — every conditional statement is grounds for rejection as non-conforming. The offer must be unambiguous.
- **Pricing in any document EXCEPT the Schedule of Prices** — never put a number in the technical volume that contradicts the Schedule of Prices.
