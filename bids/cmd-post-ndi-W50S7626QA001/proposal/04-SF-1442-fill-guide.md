# SF 1442 — section-by-section fill guide

The SF 1442 (Solicitation, Offer, and Award — Construction, Alteration, or Repair) is a federal standard form used as the cover instrument for construction solicitations. The Offeror's responsibility is to fill the **OFFER** portion (Blocks 14–20c). Per Section L paragraph (a)(1):

> *"Standard Form 1442 completed as follows:
> Complete Blocks: 14, 15, 17, 18, and 20a-c
> Complete Blocks 16 and 19 if necessary"*

## A. SF 1442 Block-by-block

### Block 1 — Solicitation Number

**Pre-filled by Government.** Verify shows: `W50S7626QA001`

### Block 2 — Type of Solicitation

**Pre-filled by Government.** Should show **Negotiated (RFP) Request for Proposal**. \[VERIFY VS COVER PAGE\]

### Block 3 — Date Issued

**Pre-filled by Government.** Should show **03 MAY 2026**.

### Block 4 — Contract Number

Leave blank. Government completes at award.

### Block 5 — Requisition / Purchase Request Number

**Pre-filled by Government.** \[VERIFY VS COVER PAGE\]

### Block 6 — Project Number

**Pre-filled by Government.** Should show **DDPM262101**.

### Block 7 — Issued By + CODE

**Pre-filled by Government.** Should show 136 AW MSC (or equivalent). DoDAAC = `W50S76`.

### Block 8 — Address Offer To

**Pre-filled by Government.** Submit to per Section L (a)(2): email `136.AW.MSC@us.af.mil` (digital submission, not paper to mailing address).

### Block 9 — For Information Call

**Pre-filled by Government.** Likely Capt Crabtree per SOW §13.1.

### Block 10 — Performance of the Work Description

**Pre-filled by Government.** Title / identifying number / date.

### Block 11 — Performance period

Verify the start clause (e.g., "begin within 10 days after award" + "complete within 90 calendar days after receiving NTP"). \[VERIFY VS COVER PAGE\]

### Block 12a — Performance and Payment Bonds Required

**\[VERIFY VS COVER PAGE\]** — should be **YES** per FAR 52.228-15.

### Block 12b — Calendar days after award to furnish bonds

**\[VERIFY VS COVER PAGE\]** — typical 10 calendar days.

### Block 13 — Solicitation requirements

**\[VERIFY VS COVER PAGE\]** — date and hour offers due. Should show **04 JUN 2026 [HOUR — likely 1500 CDT]**.

#### Block 13a — Number of copies required

**\[VERIFY\]** — but per Section L (a)(2) submission is by EMAIL, so paper copies are not in play. Likely 1 electronic.

#### Block 13B — Offer guarantee required

**\[VERIFY VS COVER PAGE — SHOULD BE YES per Section L (a)(2) NOTE\]**.

#### Block 13d — Min acceptance period (calendar days)

**\[VERIFY\]** — typical 90 days. If left blank, default per the SF 1442 instructions to whatever Block 17 says. To be safe, **enter 90 in Block 17**.

### Block 14 — Name and Address of Offeror (incl. ZIP)

**OFFEROR FILLS.** Use SAM-registered legal entity name + SAM-registered address.

```
Block 14 — NAME AND ADDRESS OF OFFEROR
[Firm legal name as registered in SAM]
[Street address]
[City, State, ZIP]

CODE: [USER TO FILL — typically the firm's CAGE]
FACILITY CODE: [USER TO FILL — typically the firm's facility code or leave blank]
```

### Block 15 — Telephone Number

**OFFEROR FILLS.** Include area code, no collect calls.

```
Block 15 — TELEPHONE NUMBER (Include area code)
([Area]) [###]-[####]
```

### Block 16 — Remittance Address

**OFFEROR FILLS** *only if different from Block 14.* If same, leave blank.

### Block 17 — Acceptance period

**OFFEROR FILLS.** Insert any number ≥ Block 13d minimum (typical 90).

```
Block 17 — The offeror agrees to perform the work required at the prices
specified below in strict accordance with the terms of this solicitation,
if this offer is accepted by the Government in writing within
[90] calendar days after the date offers are due.
```

> Default to **90** unless your bid pricing is volatile or you need a longer hold-open period (rare).

### Block 18 — Furnishing Bonds

**OFFEROR FILLS.**

```
Block 18 — The offeror agrees to furnish any required performance and
payment bonds.
```

Confirm via signature in Block 20.

### Block 19 — Acknowledgment of Amendments

**OFFEROR FILLS** if any amendments. There is **one amendment** (Amendment 0001):

```
Block 19 — ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF AMENDMENTS
AMENDMENT NUMBER     DATE
W50S7626QA0010001    [VERIFY VS SF 30 BLOCK 16C — likely 2026-05-12]
```

> **Critical to fill.** Per FAR 14.405 + SF 30 instructions, *"Failure of your acknowledgment to be received at the place designated for the receipt of offers prior to the hour and date specified may result in rejection of your offer."* Acknowledge in Block 19 OR by returning a completed SF 30 with Blocks 8 + 15 completed. Don't leave this blank.

### Block 20a — Name and Title of Person Authorized to Sign Offer

**OFFEROR FILLS.** Print name and title:

```
Block 20a — NAME AND TITLE OF PERSON AUTHORIZED TO SIGN OFFER
[Authorized Officer name]
[Title — President / VP / Officer / etc.]
```

### Block 20b — Signature

**OFFEROR SIGNS.** Wet-ink signature on the printed form, then scan to PDF.

### Block 20c — Offer Date

**OFFEROR FILLS.** Date of submission (= date the email is sent — Thu 2026-06-04).

### Block 21 — Items Accepted

Government completes at award.

### Block 22 — Amount

**OFFEROR FILLS.** Total bid amount (sum of CLIN 0001 + CLIN 0002).

```
Block 22 — AMOUNT
$[USER TO FILL]
(See Schedule of Prices for CLIN-by-CLIN breakdown)
```

### Block 23 — Accounting and Appropriation Data

Government completes at award.

### Block 24 — Submit Invoices to Address Shown In

**Pre-filled by Government.** Per Section G WAWF clause — submit invoices via WAWF (DoDAAC `F87700` Pay Official; `W50S76` Issue/Admin; `F6J3CE` Service Approver/Acceptor).

### Block 25 — Other than Full and Open Competition Pursuant to USC

N/A for this set-aside acquisition.

### Block 26 — Administered By

**Pre-filled by Government.** DoDAAC `W50S76` per Section G.

### Block 27 — Payment Will Be Made By

**Pre-filled by Government.** DoDAAC `F87700` per Section G WAWF.

### Block 28 — Negotiated Agreement

Government completes at award.

### Block 29 — Award

Government completes at award.

### Block 30a–30c

Government completes at award (acceptance signature).

### Block 31a–31c

Government completes at award (CO signature).

## B. Filled-form summary (the Offeror's contribution)

After fill, the SF 1442 should show:

| Block | Filled? | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 14 | YES | Firm legal name + address + CODE (CAGE) |
| 15 | YES | Phone |
| 16 | NO unless different | (blank if remittance = Block 14) |
| 17 | YES | 90 (or longer) |
| 18 | YES (signature confirms) | (no separate text fill) |
| 19 | YES | Amendment 0001 + date |
| 20a | YES | Authorized officer name + title |
| 20b | YES | Signature (wet-ink) |
| 20c | YES | Offer date |
| 22 | YES | Total bid amount |

## C. Common errors

| Error | Consequence | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Block 14 firm name doesn't match SAM-registered name | Rejected as non-conforming | Cross-check SAM exactly before fill |
| Block 17 left blank | Defaults to Block 13d minimum | Always enter 90 (or higher if pricing volatile) |
| Block 19 amendment not acknowledged | "May result in rejection of your offer" per SF 30 | Always acknowledge amendments in Block 19 |
| Block 20a signed without printed name above | Sometimes rejected, sometimes corrected by CO | Always print name + title before signing |
| Block 22 amount doesn't match Schedule of Prices total | CO may flag for clarification | Reconcile before signing |
| Wet-ink signature followed by scan with low resolution | Sometimes rejected as non-original signature | Use ≥ 300 DPI scan; preserve original wet-ink original on file |

## D. Final assembly

1. Print blank SF 1442 (the Government-issued PDF)
2. Type or hand-write Blocks 14, 15, (16), 17, 19, 20a, 20c, 22
3. Authorized officer signs Block 20b in wet ink
4. Scan completed form to PDF at ≥ 300 DPI
5. Save filename: `W50S7626QA001_SF1442_[Firm Name]_[date].pdf`
6. Verify the scan is legible (zoom in on each block)
7. Attach to submission email (`10-submission-checklist.md`)

## E. Block 13b — Sealed bid envelope marking

Per Section L paragraph (a)(2), submission is by email — **no sealed envelope is required**. The Block 13B language about "Sealed envelopes containing offers shall be marked to show the offeror's name and address, the solicitation number, and the date and time offers are due" is N/A for email submission. The submission email subject line accomplishes the equivalent labeling per `10-submission-checklist.md`.
