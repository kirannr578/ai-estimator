## F. Use during pricing

1. Update `takeoff-template.json` quantities with Section II.B and Section III.B "Qty After Visit" values.
2. Re-run `05-bid-schedule-mapping.md` § C markup envelope with updated direct costs.
3. Adjust `takeoff-template.json` `_meta.contingency_pct_clin_0001` and `_meta.contingency_pct_clin_0002` per Section IV decisions.
4. Re-confirm bid bond amount with bonding agent if total moves > 10% from prior estimate.
5. Update `01-price-proposal.md` § C/D internal cost build-ups.

## G. Filing

Attach the completed memo to the firm-internal bid file. **Do NOT include in the proposal package** — site-visit memos are internal working documents. The proposal carries only the SF 1442 + Schedule of Prices + Subfactor 1 + Subfactor 2 + (optional past-perf, bondability letter, key personnel).

If the CO asks at evaluation whether the offeror visited the site, be ready to confirm Yes (or describe the alternative — e.g., field-verify post-award per FAR 52.236-3).
