## B. Six questions

### Question 1 — Closing date and time

**Reference**: SF 1442 Block 13.

**Question**: We have read the closing date as **04 June 2026** from the form-field value of SF 1442 Block 13. Could the Government please confirm:
- (a) The exact closing date of 04 June 2026, AND
- (b) The exact closing hour and time zone (e.g., 1500 CDT)?

> Internal note: This question is critical because the form-field hour did not extract reliably from our PDF text-layer pass. **A 5-minute visual confirmation by the firm in Adobe Reader resolves this risk independent of the CO's response.** Send only if internal verification leaves residual uncertainty.

### Question 2 — Site-visit accommodation

**Reference**: SOW §4.3 (site visit "urged" prior to offer submission), §7 (14-business-day advance access request rule).

**Question**: SOW §4.3 urges site visits prior to offer submission. SOW §7 requires base-access requests no less than 14 business days prior to access. With the closing date 12 calendar days from today, a standard-rule pre-bid site walk of B1672 and B1675 is administratively impractical. We request the Government's accommodation in one of two forms:
- (a) Issue a one-time accelerated-processing exception for an escorted 30-minute walk through B1672 + B1675 by no more than 2 Blue Print Constructs personnel between Wednesday 28 May 2026 and Wednesday 03 June 2026, OR
- (b) Confirm that an offeror submitting on field-verified-after-award basis (per FAR 52.236-3) will not be disadvantaged in the comparative evaluation.

We acknowledge that the choice between (a) and (b) is the Government's. Identifying contact: [PM name + email + phone].

### Question 3 — B1675 NDI Room scope confirmation

**Reference**: SOW §1.2 + §2.2; Drawings Sheet 2 of 2.

**Question**: SOW §1.2 specifies "convert a former and decommissioned Aqueous Film Forming Foam (AFFF) room in Building B1675 for use as a NDI facility." SOW §2.2 enumerates the trade scope as "interior wall demolition and construction, painting, caulking, carpentry, and associated electrical, mechanical, and plumbing work." We have interpreted CLIN 0002 as a **space-conversion of B1675 to NDI-suitable space**, with NDI equipment to be installed by the Government post-renovation, and **no contractor scope for radiation shielding (lead-line), EMI/RF shielding, vibration isolation, or specialty NDI environmental controls**. Could the Government please confirm whether this interpretation is correct, and if not, identify the specialty-shielding or specialty-environmental scope that is contractor-furnished under CLIN 0002.

### Question 4 — Existing fire-suppression and HVAC system documentation

**Reference**: Drawing General Note 9 (modify current fire-suppression to encompass new partitions); Drawing General Note 10 (balance HVAC); SOW §4.4 (drawings/as-builts for reference only).

**Question**: To enable accurate sub-quoting for fire-suppression and HVAC modification:
- (a) What is the **manufacturer + model + system type (wet-pipe / dry-pipe / pre-action)** of the existing fire-suppression system serving B1672 and B1675?
- (b) What is the **manufacturer + control-platform** of the existing HVAC building management system (e.g., Trane Tracer, Honeywell, Siemens, BACnet generic) at B1672 and B1675, for purposes of integrating the in-room HVAC override button?
- (c) Can the Government make existing as-built drawings or system documentation available, or alternatively, schedule a maintenance-staff Q&A call between Blue Print Constructs and the relevant 136 CES facility-services point of contact?

### Question 5 — AFFF-residue handling on B1675 demolition

**Reference**: SOW §1.2 (former AFFF room, decommissioned); §9 (Disposal); 136 Environmental Office.

**Question**: SOW §1.2 indicates the AFFF system is "decommissioned." For purposes of B1675 demolition pricing:
- (a) Has the AFFF-impacted substrate (concrete coatings, sumps, piping headers, discharge nozzles, floor drains) been remediated to non-PFAS-impacted condition? OR
- (b) Should the contractor anticipate **PFAS-conscious demolition** with TCEQ-permitted disposal beyond the SOW §9 standard universal-waste handling for paint-related materials?

If (b), is the 136 Environmental Office the operational coordination point, and is a specialty environmental sub anticipated?

### Question 6 — NAICS code confirmation

**Reference**: SF 1442 cover page NAICS field; FAR 52.219-6 set-aside.

**Question**: We have read the applicable NAICS code as **236220 — Commercial and Institutional Building Construction, $45.0M small-business size standard** based on the Product Service Code Z2AA and the scope of work. Could the Government please confirm the assigned NAICS code on the SF 1442 cover page, so that our SAM.gov small-business size assertion at the registered NAICS aligns precisely with this solicitation?

> Internal note: This question is also resolvable via 5-minute visual review of the SF 1442 cover page. Send only if uncertainty persists.
