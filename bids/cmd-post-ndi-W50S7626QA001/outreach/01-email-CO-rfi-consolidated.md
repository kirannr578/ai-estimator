painting, caulking, carpentry, and associated electrical, mechanical, and
plumbing work." We have interpreted CLIN 0002 as a space-conversion of
B1675 to NDI-suitable space, with NDI equipment to be installed by the
Government post-renovation, and no contractor scope for radiation
shielding, EMI/RF shielding, vibration isolation, or specialty NDI
environmental controls. Could the Government confirm whether this
interpretation is correct?

(b) Manufacturer/control-platform of the existing HVAC BMS at B1672 and
    B1675?
(c) Existing as-built drawings or system documentation, OR a Q&A call
    with the relevant 136 CES facility-services point of contact?

floor drains) been remediated to non-PFAS-impacted condition? If not,
should the contractor anticipate PFAS-conscious demolition with
TCEQ-permitted disposal beyond the SOW §9 standard handling? Is the
136 Environmental Office the operational coordination point?

small-business size standard, based on PSC Z2AA and the scope of work.

cover page so that our SAM.gov small-business size assertion aligns
precisely with this solicitation?
