# FAR / DFARS clauses worth flagging

The full clause-by-clause list is in `Solicitation - W50S7626QA001 Cmd Post and NDI.pdf` Sections G, I, K, and L. This document calls out the **clauses with material consequence to bid decisions** — bonding, wages, set-aside, and federal-procedural clauses that drive submission compliance.

## A. Bonds + Bid Guarantee (Section L + Section I)

| Clause | Title | Effect on bid |
|---|---|---|
| **FAR 52.228-1** | Bid Guarantee (Sept 1996) | **Bid bond required with offer** if Block 13B of SF 1442 is checked YES (verify visually). Amount = **20% of bid OR $3,000,000, whichever is less.** Form: SF 24, supported by T-Circular-570 surety, or postal money order, certified check, cashier's check, irrevocable letter of credit, or qualifying Treasury bonds. **Failure to furnish a bid guarantee in proper form may be cause for rejection of the bid.** Section L paragraph (a)(2) NOTE: scanned copy of the bid bond must be attached to the submission email. |
| **FAR 52.228-15 (Deviation 2020-O0016)** | Performance and Payment Bonds — Construction (Apr 2020) | If award **> $150,000**, performance bond at **100%** of contract price (SF 25) AND payment bond at **100%** of contract price (SF 25A). Both furnished within the time frame in the Bid Guarantee provision (typically 10 days after award notice). At likely award magnitude ($300K–$700K), bonds are virtually certain. |
| **FAR 52.228-2** | Additional Bond Security | If contract price increases via mod, additional bond protection generally equal to 100% of price increase. |
| **FAR 52.228-5** | Insurance — Work on a Government Installation | Floor: WC $100K, GL $500K BI/occ, Auto $200K/$500K BI + $20K PD (per Section L paragraph (g) verbatim). |
| **FAR 52.228-11 (Deviation 2020-O0016)** | Pledges of Assets | Mechanism for pledging non-cash assets in lieu of bond — only relevant if firm uses individual surety vs. corporate surety. Most firms use Treasury Circular 570 corporate surety. |
| **FAR 52.228-12** | Prospective Subcontractor Requests for Bonds | Sub-tier bond mechanism; minor admin |
| **FAR 52.228-14** | Irrevocable Letter of Credit | Acceptable bond alternative |
| **FAR 52.228-17** | Individual Surety — Pledge of Assets (Bid Guarantee) | If using individual surety on bid bond, follow asset-pledge mechanics |

## J. The 4 procedural deviations to NOT confuse

The solicitation invokes **DEVIATION 2026-O0038** on most FAR/DFARS clauses. These deviations were issued by DoD in early 2026 to address:
- Removal of certain DEI-related language requirements (52.222-90 was added)
- Streamlining of Annual Reps & Certs (DFARS 252.204-7998 Alt A)
- Updating WD reference mechanics (52.222-6)

For practical purposes, treat the deviated clauses as the same operational requirement as their non-deviated parents, noting that the **Apr 2026** language version of each clause is what governs (more recent than the prior FAR Council edits).

## L. Summary — top 5 clauses with bid-decision impact

1. **FAR 52.219-6** — Total Small Business Set-Aside → must be SAM-registered as small at NAICS 236220
2. **FAR 52.228-1** — Bid Guarantee → bid bond at 20% of bid (or $3M cap) due **WITH** the offer
3. **FAR 52.228-15** — P&P Bonds → 100%/100% bonds due 10 days after award notice
4. **FAR 52.222-6** — Davis-Bacon → WH-347 weekly certified payroll; pay TX20260270 rates
5. **DFARS 252.204-7019** — NIST 800-171 SPRS Basic Assessment → must be on file in SPRS prior to award
