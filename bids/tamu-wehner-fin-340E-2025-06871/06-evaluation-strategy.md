### Tactic 2: Pricing realism vs. aggressiveness

- Use TAMU System-area cost data (Brazos County prevailing wage + typical TAMU specialty-sub rates)

- Avoid "too expensive" pricing — TAMU's published budget range (TBD from CSP package) should anchor the midrange
