### MARRS glass wall

```
Q1. MARRS Wall System variant

The SOW references "MARRS glass wall along corridor." Drawings show [confirm
from drawings].

Question: Is a specific MARRS Wall System variant (e.g., Modernfold MARRS 8000
Series, frame depth, glass thickness, hardware) required? Or is contractor's

Why it matters: MARRS Wall System pricing varies $300-$600 per LF depending on
variant + finish + hardware. Confirmation prevents bid-level rework.
```
