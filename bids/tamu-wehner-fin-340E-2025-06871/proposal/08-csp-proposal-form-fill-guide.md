## 1. Form structure (CSI-Division Pricing Table)

TAMU System CSP Proposal Forms typically present a table with one row per CSI Division. The bidder enters a **dollar amount per CSI Division that is in scope**, OR marks the row **BLANK** for divisions not in scope. **Do NOT enter $0 for blank scope** — that's interpreted as $0 cost AND being responsible for that scope.

| Field | Format |
|---|---|
| CSI Division | Pre-populated |
| Division Name | Pre-populated |
| Bidder's Amount ($) | Bidder enters |
