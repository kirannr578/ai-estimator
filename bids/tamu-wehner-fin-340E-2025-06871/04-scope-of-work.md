## Quantity-takeoff strategy

Once drawings are in hand:

1. **Open A2.x (Floor Plans) in Bluebeam** — measure room SF for all 4 combined rooms; mark up demo + new partitions in LF.
2. **Open A3 (Ceiling Plan)** — count diffusers, light fixtures; measure new ceiling SF.
3. **Open A7 (MARRS detail)** — measure glass-wall LF + height = SF; identify door count.
4. **Open S1-S2 (Structural)** — confirm whether any walls are load-bearing (will dictate sub trade scope).
5. **Open M1, E1, P1** — count diffusers, switches, outlets, plumbing fixtures.
6. **Populate `takeoff-template.json`** with measured quantities + sub-quote unit costs.
