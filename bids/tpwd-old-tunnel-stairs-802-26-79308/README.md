## Why this opportunity

1. **Scope fit (good).** Stair replacement at a state park is a clear, time-boxed scope. The 48-page IFB includes Attachments B (Specifications + Prevailing Wage), C (Drawings), D (Qualifications), E (Bid Schedule) — single bound document. BPC self-performs framing + finish carpentry; light civil if any pad work is required.
2. **Magnitude.** Not explicitly stated, but the **5% bid-bond rule applies only if bid > $25,000** (§15 of the IFB). For a 30-day-POP stair replacement in a state park, expect bid range **$15K – $75K**. This is at or below BPC's small-project comfort zone.
3. **Schedule.** Tight — 30 days from NTP. Old Tunnel State Park is a small day-use park (famous for bat colonies / sunset bat flights) with seasonal visitor traffic; phasing matters.
4. **Geography.** Fredericksburg / Kendall County — Texas Hill Country, ~4-5 hr drive from BPC Frisco. Site supervision is manageable.

## Headline characteristics

- **Site visit** is *non-mandatory* but "strongly encouraged" (§3). Schedule with Park Superintendent Elizabeth (Izzy) Mabry — Phone 830-765-8101, Email `Elizabeth.mabry@tpwd.texas.gov`. **Attendees should be prepared to tour by foot with proper clothing and footwear** — the "lower loop" is a trail-system stair.
- **Insurance** is heavy for a small contract: WC statutory, Employer's Liability $1M/$1M/$1M, **CGL $1M occurrence / $2M aggregate / $2M products-completed**, Auto $1M CSL. State of Texas / TPWD as **additional insured AND loss payee** (§11.3). COI due within 10 days of award (§11.4).
- **LD rate** is $500/week — modest by federal-construction standards but penal at the bid magnitude.
- **Retainage** 10% of contract price withheld pending warranty + O&M documentation (§23).
- **One-year warranty** on material and labor (§4.1.4).
- **Best Value** evaluation (§3.3 of Attachment A General T&Cs) — TPWD weights past performance via the CPA Vendor Performance Tracking System.
