## Working hypothesis — likely scope categories

Veterans-memorial *expansion* projects at North-Texas municipal parks typically include some mix of the following. Each line is annotated with whether BPC can self-perform or should sub. Pricing-by-line will require the actual IFB drawings.

| Category | Typical scope | BPC self-perform vs. sub | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Demolition / site prep** | Selective demo of existing memorial perimeter, removal of failed pavers, sodcutting, grub & strip topsoil | Self (light demo) | Quantify against IFB drawings |
| **Site grading & drainage** | Re-grade for ADA-compliant approach, French drains around new hardscape | Sub (civil) | Engage 1–2 local civil subs |
| **Hardscape (concrete)** | New concrete plaza, walkways, ADA-compliant ramps, possibly stamped/colored concrete around the memorial | Self (BPC concrete capacity from Lavon RV Park) | Confirm finish (broom, exposed-aggregate, stamped) |
| **Hardscape (masonry / stone)** | Stone or brick wall expansion, seat walls, plaque pedestals, possibly a granite or bronze monument base | Sub (mason) | Memorial monuments are typically owner-furnished; BPC installs the base only |
| **Monument fabrication** | Granite / bronze plaques, name walls, sculpture pedestal | **Owner-furnished or specialty sub** | Verify in IFB whether monument is OFCI (owner-furnished, contractor-installed) or fabricated under the contract |
| **Site electrical / lighting** | LED memorial lighting, uplights, flagpole lighting, junction boxes for plaque illumination | Sub (electrical) | Confirm voltage & control (24V vs. line, photocell vs. timeclock) |
| **Flagpole** | Possibly new flagpole(s) — aluminum or fiberglass, 25–35 ft, with internal/external halyard | Sub | If included, specify in bid |
| **Site furnishings** | Benches, trash receptacles, bike racks, interpretive signage | Sub or direct purchase | OFCI vs. CFCI per IFB |
| **Landscaping** | New planting beds, sod restoration around hardscape, irrigation tie-in / extension | Sub (landscape) | Confirm if irrigation extension is in scope |
| **Restoration / sodding** | Restore disturbed areas to original condition | Sub (landscape) | |
| **Site signage** | Memorial dedication signage, interpretive panels | Sub (specialty signage) | OFCI possible |

## What's **not** likely in scope

- Major utility relocations (water/sewer mains) — Bethany Lakes Park is established
- Vertical building construction (no buildings mentioned in the scope summary)
- Asbestos/lead abatement (no existing building to demo)
- Environmental remediation
- Major civil earthwork

## Critical questions to resolve at the pre-bid meeting (2026-06-02)

1. **Magnitude.** Engineer's estimate or budget range?
2. **Drawings vs. design-build.** Are full drawings provided, or does the scope include any design responsibility?
3. **Monument fabrication.** OFCI, OFOI, or CFCI?
4. **Schedule.** Substantial completion target? Memorial Day 2027 readiness?
5. **Phasing / site access.** Is Bethany Lakes Park open during construction? Are there event blackout dates (Memorial Day weekend, Veterans Day, July 4)?
6. **Allowance lines.** Are there any allowance line items (landscaping, signage, monument)?
7. **Alternates.** Are bid alternates priced (e.g., upgrade to bronze plaques vs. aluminum)?
8. **Prevailing wage.** Confirm Collin County prevailing wage schedule applies (Tex. Gov't Code Ch. 2258).
