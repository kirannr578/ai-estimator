# {{PROJECT_NAME}} — FAR Clauses Flags (SAP Best-Value Variant)

> **Use:** Map every FAR clause in RFQ Section I + Section H to the impact on BPC pricing, execution, and submittal obligations. The clauses below are the **typical** federal SAP best-value inventory; refine per RFQ.
> SAP rides a **lighter** clause inventory than LPTA / FAR 15 — note the absence of most FAR 52.215 source-selection clauses and the typical presence of the 52.213 series (and 52.212 series if commercial-item).

## 3. Clauses NOT typical on SAP (vs. full FAR Part 15)

The following clauses appear regularly in LPTA / FAR 15 RFPs but are **not typical** in SAP RFQs:

| Clause | Title | Why absent on SAP |
|---|---|---|
| FAR 52.215-1 | Instructions to Offerors — Competitive Acquisition | FAR Part 15 source-selection clause; SAP uses 52.212-1 / 52.213-4 instead |
| FAR 52.215-2 | Audit and Records | Tied to Part 15 cost-or-pricing data; rare at SAP scale |
| FAR 52.215-22/-23 | Pass-Through Charges | Tied to Part 15 |
| FAR 52.219-9 | Small Business Subcontracting Plan | Triggers > $750K for non-small primes; rare on sub-SAT SAP |
| FAR 52.222-22 / -25 | Affirmative Action / EEO | Triggers > 50 employees + EEO threshold; verify if BPC is over |

If any of these DO appear in the RFQ, the CO has elected fuller source-selection mechanics — re-confirm the template archetype is correct.

## 5. Pre-submission action checklist (from clause review)

- [ ] SAM.gov registration current (52.204-7)
- [ ] Reps & Certs current ≤ 12 months (52.204-8 / 52.212-3)
- [ ] Section 889 reps current in SAM (52.204-24/-25/-26)
- [ ] FASCSA reps current (52.204-29)
- [ ] Small-business size assertion verified (52.219-1)
- [ ] Bid bond status verified — typically NOT required on SAP per Section I; if 52.228-1 present, order SF-24
- [ ] Alternative payment protection commitment letter ordered (52.228-13) — post-award due within 10 cal days
- [ ] Buy American certificates signed (52.225-2, 52.225-4) if RFQ triggers
- [ ] Wage Determination transcribed into labor rate sheet (52.222-6) — applies regardless of SAT
- [ ] Self-perform ≥ 15% of direct cost verified (52.236-1)
- [ ] Safety + QC + Schedule plan starter drafts on file for post-award submittal (52.236-13, 52.236-15, 52.246-12)
- [ ] Insurance limits verified against agency-specific clause (e.g. DOI 1452.228-70 = $100K/$500K/$500K)
