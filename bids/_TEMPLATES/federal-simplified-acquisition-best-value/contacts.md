## Communication norms

- **CO is the formal contracting authority.** All formal correspondence (RFIs, RFI responses, change orders, etc.) goes to / from the CO.
- **CS handles administrative coordination.** Day-to-day questions can go to the CS; formal questions go to the CO.
- **Site POC is for site-visit logistics + post-award site coordination.** Do not direct contracting questions to the Site POC.
- **Email is the primary channel.** Phone calls should be followed up in email to create a record.
- **Multiple addresses for the same person sometimes appear in federal RFQs.** If so, send to both and CC the CO; raise as RFI.
- **SAP-specific:** the CO is often **not named** in the RFQ — only the Contract Specialist appears, with the CO designated at award. Address formal communication to the named CS until the CO is identified.
