## Volume III — Past Performance (if separated)

Some RFPs split past performance into its own volume. If so, move past-performance deliverables from Volume II to Volume III; otherwise leave in Volume II.

## What NOT to submit (LPTA discipline)

- Schedule narrative (post-award submittal)
- Safety plan (post-award submittal per FAR 52.236-13)
- QC plan (post-award submittal)
- Subcontractor management plan (post-award submittal)
- Closeout plan (post-award submittal)
- Unsolicited alternates (non-responsive)
- "Why us" marketing pitch (not scored)
- Any technical narrative beyond bare technical acceptability

## Post-award deliverables (for awareness, not for proposal)

The following are due **after award** and should be priced into General Conditions but **not included in the proposal**:

- NTP+5: Preconstruction conference attendance per FAR 52.236-26
- NTP+10: Initial baseline CPM schedule (per FAR 52.236-15 / spec)
- NTP+14: Site-specific safety plan (per FAR 52.236-13 + USACE EM 385-1-1 if USACE)
- NTP+14: QC Plan (per spec)
- Weekly: WH-347 Certified Payroll (prime + every sub tier)
- Monthly: Pay app + lien waivers
- Substantial completion: closeout package per FAR 52.246-12 / spec
