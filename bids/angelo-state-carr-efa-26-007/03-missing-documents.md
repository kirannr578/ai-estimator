- Items #5, #6 are eligibility / coordination items → resolve via Samuel by 2026-05-23.

- Items #14, #15, #18, #19 are risk-reducers; their absence is acceptable if explicitly flagged in the proposal's Assumptions & Clarifications, but they meaningfully tighten the price.
