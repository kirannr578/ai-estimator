## E. Refined pricing scenarios with actual 3,115 SF footprint (2026-05-23 refresh)

### Scenario A — Bid-leader (aggressive)
- 1,047 SF back-of-house @ $235/SF + 1,739 SF corridor/entry @ $65/SF + 129 SF mech/elev @ $30/SF = $246K + $113K + $4K = $363K direct
- × 1.078 contingency × 1.10 OH × 1.06 profit = **$455K all-in**
- Wins on price if competitor pricing clusters >$475K. Margin is tight; demands clean execution.

### Scenario B — Bid-center (most likely sweet spot)
- $265/SF back-of-house + $90/SF corridor/entry + $30/SF mech/elev = $277K + $156K + $4K = $437K direct
- × 1.078 × 1.10 × 1.06 = **$549K all-in** — ABOVE budget
- *Revise down by VE on the corridor (lower-cost carpet tile + paint-only) to get to ~$485K. This is the realistic target.*

### Scenario C — Bid-conservative (full envelope)
- $310/SF back-of-house + $115/SF corridor/entry + $30/SF mech/elev = $325K + $200K + $4K = $529K direct
- × 1.078 × 1.10 × 1.06 = **$665K all-in** — well above budget
- Triggers VE conversation or alternate proposal. Surface to user + Hannah Bignall immediately if drawings push us to this scenario.

**Recommended target: Scenario A-B blend at $465K-$485K** — competitive against the 4 known competitors (Ardent United / JAJ / Onyx / Collier) while preserving margin for the active-building risk premium.
