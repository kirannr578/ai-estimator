## D. Two-person QC pass (2026-06-04, the day before proposal delivery)

The 2-person QC pass uses two reviewers: the primary preparer + a second reviewer who has NOT been involved in package assembly. Two passes:

### Pass 1 — Document-level QC (each item on the checklist above)

For every item A1–A20 in § A.1 AND B1–B5 in § B.1:
- [ ] Item is physically present in the envelope
- [ ] Item is the original (not a photocopy)
- [ ] Item has all required signatures in blue ink
- [ ] Item is current (W-9 current calendar year, ACORDs within 30 days, HUB cert within 2 years, etc.)

### Pass 2 — Cross-document consistency QC

The single biggest cause of TTUS clerk-level rejection is **legal-name mismatch across documents**. Verify the firm's exact legal name is identical across:

- [ ] Attachment A (Form of Proposal)
- [ ] Cover letter
- [ ] Bid bond
- [ ] Surety's power of attorney
- [ ] Insurance ACORDs (named insured)
- [ ] W-9
- [ ] CIQ
- [ ] HB 1295 (filing entity name)
- [ ] HSP form (Section 1 respondent name)
- [ ] TX Comptroller good-standing printout

And:

- [ ] FEIN matches across Attachment A, W-9, HSP form
- [ ] TX TIN matches across Attachment A, HSP form, TX Comptroller printout
- [ ] Base proposal price spelled in words matches the figure
- [ ] Total proposal = Base + $25K allowance
- [ ] All addenda acknowledged in Attachment A AND in the cover letter
- [ ] HSP commitment % matches the commitment cited in the cover letter ("30% of subcontracted dollars")
- [ ] HSP self-performance % matches `../05-hsp-plan.md` § B
- [ ] Schedule narrative milestones match the CPM Gantt exhibit
- [ ] Submittal log includes long-lead items per `05-schedule-narrative.md` § 4
- [ ] References in `04-past-performance.md` have current contact info verified within 7 days

Second reviewer initials each item; both reviewers sign the bottom of the checklist.
