## C. Construction phase (2026-07-01 → 2026-11-02)

> Detailed CPM Gantt to be developed as a separate exhibit with the technical proposal. The high-level milestones below assume a typical TX higher-ed interior renovation sequence.

| Week | Phase | Key activities |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-NTP (6/22 – 6/30) | Mobilization prep | Order long-lead items (Sargent locks, theater millwork, theater-grade lighting fixtures), submittals to A/E |
| Week 1–2 (7/1 – 7/14) | Mobilize + protect adjacent + CBORD/IT coordination call + EHS shutdown coordination + hazmat survey review | Site mobilization, dust containment install, walk-off mats, protection of adjacent spaces |
| Week 3–4 (7/15 – 7/28) | Demolition | Selective demo of finishes + casework + ceiling + MEP terminations; hazmat abatement if triggered |
| Week 5–8 (7/29 – 8/25) | MEP rough-in | HVAC ductwork + plumbing supply/waste/vent + electrical conduit + low-voltage pathway + CBORD prep at doors |
| Week 9–11 (8/26 – 9/15) | In-wall + above-ceiling close-out | Insulation, blocking, fire-stopping, GWB hung; ceiling grid set; in-wall inspections; brick restoration if applicable |
| Week 12–14 (9/16 – 10/06) | Finishes | Flooring (sheet vinyl, LVT, tile in wet zones), painting, finish carpentry trim, millwork install (theater dressing-room vanities + mirrors), ceiling tile, door + frame + Sargent hardware install |
| Week 15–16 (10/07 – 10/20) | MEP trim + final | Light fixtures, diffusers/grilles, plumbing fixture set, electrical devices, controls, signage, accessories, fire-alarm device install |
| Week 17 (10/21 – 10/27) | TAB + commissioning + owner training | Test/adjust/balance HVAC; commissioning of controls; owner training on systems |
| Week 18 (10/28 – 11/02) | Punch + substantial completion | Walk punch list with Samuel + A/E; address punch items; **substantial completion 11/2 target with float against $250/day LD** |
| Week 19–22 (11/03 – 11/30) | Close-out | Final punch closure, record drawings, O&M manuals, attic stock delivery, final clean, final pay app |

**Critical-path drivers (the items most likely to slip the schedule):**
1. **Long-lead millwork (theater dressing-room vanities + LED mirror surrounds)** — 10–14 week lead time; must order on or before 7/14 to land by week 12
2. **Sargent cylindrical locks** — 8–14 week lead time depending on series; order on NTP
3. **Owner-furnished item handoffs** — owner's tech/cabling/FP/equipment vendors need access in the right sequence; coordination meetings at end of week 8 + end of week 12 + end of week 16
4. **EHS shutdown windows** — fire alarm + HVAC cutover; request 2 weeks ahead
5. **A/E RFI turnaround** — depending on A/E firm; track with weekly RFI log
