## Why this matters

Per FAR 52.215-1(c)(3), proposals received at the proper address by the deadline are conforming. Proposals received at an alternate address — even if the alternate address is plausible — may be deemed non-conforming on a strict reading. The dual-address strategy ensures the proposal reaches the operative inbox regardless of which one the CO ultimately treats as authoritative.

The CO is unlikely to penalize an offeror for being over-inclusive on addressing; they may penalize for being under-inclusive.
