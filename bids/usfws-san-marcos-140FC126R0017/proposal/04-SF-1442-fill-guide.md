>
> The Standard Form 1442 (Solicitation, Offer, and Award) is the *one* signed form in this proposal. Blocks 14–20C are the Offeror's responsibility; the CO completes blocks 21–32 post-award.
>
> Original wet-ink signature in block 20B is the traditional default; the federal government has accepted accepted-form electronic signatures since the 2019 OMB memorandum on electronic signatures, and this RFP does not specifically require wet-ink. Use the firm's standard e-sig flow if one exists; otherwise print, sign in blue or black ink, scan at 300+ DPI, and embed in the Volume I PDF.

## D. Hard rules

1. **Every block 14–20C must be completed.** A blank block on the SF 1442 is a non-conformance.
2. **Block 19 must say "NONE" if no amendments** — do not leave it blank.
3. **Block 20B signature is the legal binding action.** The signer must have authority to bind the entity.
4. **Block 13d acceptance period ≥ 90 days.** A shorter period is non-conforming per RFP p.3.
5. **CAGE + UEI in block 17 must match SAM exactly.** Any discrepancy triggers payment-routing problems and casts doubt on responsibility.
6. **Subject line on submission email:** `Proposal Submission-140FC126R0017` (RFP p.3, exact wording). Not "Bid Submission", not "Offer for", not "RE:" — exact match.

## E. Common SF 1442 mistakes that kill bids

| Mistake | Fix |
|---|---|
| Unsigned block 20B | Re-sign and re-submit — but if past the deadline, the bid is dead. **Sign before scanning.** |
| Block 19 amendments not acknowledged | Re-pull SAM for amendments on submission morning; re-stamp SF 1442 if any new amendment landed since the last review |
| Block 13d acceptance period < 90 days | Type "90" or "120" — never leave at "60" or shorter |
| Block 17 CAGE/UEI mismatch with SAM | Re-verify against `https://sam.gov/` Entity Information |
| Block 14 entity name doesn't match SAM | Verify legal entity name; minor punctuation differences (e.g., "LLC" vs "L.L.C.") are usually fine but bigger differences trigger CO clarification questions |
| Wet-ink signature on a scanned form is illegible | Re-sign in black ink; scan at 600 DPI |
| Multiple SF 1442s in the submission package | Submit ONE signed SF 1442 in Volume I. Do not include a duplicate in Volume II. |
