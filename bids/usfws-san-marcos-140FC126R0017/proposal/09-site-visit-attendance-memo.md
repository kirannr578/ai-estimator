> Source: FAR 52.236-27 (Site Visit — Construction) + RFP p.3 + §L (site visit reference).
>
> **Use:** Pre-formatted memo template to capture findings at the **2026-05-27 OR 2026-05-28** site visit (8 AM – 4 PM CDT). The memo is firm-internal (not submitted in Volume I or II). Its outputs feed:

## D. Independent-price-determination caution

Per FAR 52.203-2: at the site visit, do **NOT** discuss pricing, scope assumptions, or bid intent with any other offeror. The site visit is for measurement and clarification only. If another offeror tries to engage on pricing, politely decline and document the attempt.

If overheard pricing discussion among other offerors, **do not act on what was overheard** but consider documenting the incident in case a post-award protest needs context.
