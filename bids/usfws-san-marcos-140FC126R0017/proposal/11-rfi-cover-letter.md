# RFI cover letter — consolidated questions to CO

> Source: RFP §L.1.3 (p.64) — RFI format requirements + cutoff 2026-06-08, 5:00 PM EDT.
>
> **Why consolidated:** Send all questions in a single email so the CO can answer once via a single SF 30 amendment, not multiple separate amendments. Federal COs strongly prefer one consolidated RFI per offeror to a stream of one-off questions.
>
> **Send by:** **Monday 2026-06-01** (one week before the 6/8 cutoff). This gives the CO 5 business days to formulate answers and issue an amendment before the cutoff. Per RFP p.3, inquiries "received after the cutoff may not receive a response."
>
> **Delivery:** Use `../outreach/01-email-tracy-gamble-rfi-consolidated.md` as the email wrapper; this file is the content. Also cc Drew Ferrall (`../outreach/02-email-drew-ferrall-rfi-cc.md`) to both of his email addresses since RFP p.3 vs L.1.2 give different ones.

---

## A. RFI format requirements (per L.1.3.a)

Each RFI must include:

1. Offeror's name
2. Offeror's representative's name, title, email, and phone
3. Clear and concise question / statement
4. References to specific solicitation passage(s), specification section(s) and paragraph(s), and/or drawing(s)

Government responds by amendment to the solicitation (L.1.3.b) — check SAM.gov for amendments daily through 6/22.

## D. Tracking RFI responses

After sending:

| Date | Action |
|---|---|
| Send + 1 business day | Confirm receipt acknowledgment from CO/CS (informal email) |
| Send + 3 business days | If no acknowledgment, polite follow-up |
| Daily through 6/22 | Check SAM.gov for solicitation amendments |
| Each amendment received | Acknowledge on SF 1442 block 19 + attach signed SF 30 to Volume I |
| 6/22 final review | Verify all amendments through that date are acknowledged |

If the CO does NOT answer RFI #2 (Section B Pelican Island carryover) or RFI #3 (CLIN 0004 quantity) before submission, note in the Volume I optional cover letter (per `01-volume-I-price-proposal.md` § A item 8) that the offeror's bid is based on the published CLINs treating CLIN 0004 as Quantity 3.
