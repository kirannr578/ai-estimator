- **All correspondence by email** to CS Drew Ferrall, with copy to CO Tracy Gamble per L.1.3
- **All submittals to COTR** (RFP H.6 + SOW §1.4.C) — but COTR is TBD until award; route through CS until then
- **Contract Number must appear on every submittal / correspondence** post-award (RFP D.2, D.3)
- **Daily progress communication** post-award: weekly photos + reports to COTR + CO (G.3); daily inspector's log signed by superintendent (G.3)

### RFI format requirements (per L.1.3.a)

Each RFI must include:

1. Offeror's name
2. Offeror's representative's name, title, email, and phone
3. Clear and concise question / statement
4. References to specific solicitation passage(s), specification section(s) and paragraph(s), and/or drawing(s)

Government responds by amendment to the solicitation (L.1.3.b) — check SAM.gov for amendments daily through June 22.

### RFI cutoff

- **2026-06-08, 5:00 PM EDT**

Inquiries received after the cutoff "may not receive a response" (RFP p.3). For protest preservation, file all RFIs at least one week before cutoff (target: 2026-06-01).
