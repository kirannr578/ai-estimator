## What's NOT in scope (confirmed by spec book ToC absence)

- ❌ **Vertical building expansion** — interior renovation only
- ❌ **Roof or envelope replacement** — Section 230532 Roof Curbs is the only roof-related spec section, and it's for HVAC equipment curbs at the existing roof (no roofing membrane / re-roof scope)
- ❌ **Major structural modifications** — no Div 03 (beyond Misc CIP for slab patching), Div 04 (no masonry), Div 05 (no steel) sections in the spec book
- ❌ **Sitework / hardscape / parking / landscaping** — no Div 31, 32, 33 sections
- ❌ **Fire-suppression sprinkler modifications** — no Div 21 in spec book (likely no partition-layout change triggers sprinkler relocation; confirm at site visit)
- ❌ **Hazardous materials abatement** — no Div 02 41 19 / 02 41 23 / 02 82 33 sections (no asbestos / lead trigger spec). However, Phase-4 of an older campus building **may have legacy materials behind the existing partitions**; BPC's quote should explicitly state inclusion-or-exclusion of hazmat survey + abatement
- ❌ **New low-voltage / data systems beyond conduit/box** — no Div 27 spec; tech sheets ET2.2/ET7.1 carry a responsibility matrix (likely OFCI cabling)

## Quoting strategy options

BPC has three viable approaches; choose at the Gate-0 decision:

### Option A — Self-perform single trade (lowest-effort quote)

Quote one of: drywall, paint, or flooring. Lowest BPC overhead, most competitive pricing on a single trade. Risk: HCS may not be looking for narrow-scope subs at this stage.

### Option B — Multi-trade self-perform package (medium effort)

Quote drywall + paint + flooring as a bundled package. BPC's natural lane and a credible MBE-sub story for HCS to roll up to MCC. Captures the bulk of the visible interior-finish trades.

### Option C — Multi-trade rolled-up sub-GC package (highest effort, highest upside)

Quote everything in the digest scope list (including managed-via-subs trades). Effectively positions BPC as a sub-GC handling the full interior reno scope, with HCS delivering pad-ready conditions. **Highest mobilization-premium exposure** given Waco distance; only viable if HCS is open to fewer, more-comprehensive subs.

**Recommend Option B post-enrichment** — drywall (092116) + paint (099123) + resilient flooring (096500/096519) is BPC's strongest self-perform stack. Add floor prep (090561) since BPC owns the floor surface. Optionally add door hardware install (087100) since BPC self-performs. Quote 087100 install at sub-cost-plus only if HCS pre-stages the doors/frames/hardware to site.

## Alternate 1

Drawing index calls out a **Base Bid + Alternate 1**. Alternate 1 sheets:

- **A6.3** ALTERNATE 1 REFLECTED CEILING PLAN
- **A7.3** ALTERNATE 1 1ST FLOOR FINISH PLAN
- **A7.4** INTERIOR ELEVATIONS / MILLWORK SECTIONS (presumed to include alt elevations)
- **MD2.1A** PARTIAL MECHANICAL 1ST DEMOLITION PLAN ALTERNATE 1
- **MH2.2A** PARTIAL MECHANICAL 1ST FLOOR PLAN ALTERNATE 1
- **ED2.1A** PARTIAL ELECTRICAL DEMOLITION 1ST FLOOR PLAN ALTERNATE 1
- **EL2.1A** PARTIAL LIGHTING 1ST FLOOR CEILING PLAN ALTERNATE 1

**Pattern**: Alternate 1 modifies finishes + ceiling + lighting + mechanical in a sub-area. Likely an "upgrade package" for select rooms (e.g., cosmetology-vs-classroom finish tier, or LED dimming/control upgrade). Pricing route: **Section 012300 Alternates** in PM. Read 012300 before pricing build to confirm Alt 1 is additive (default) vs. deductive.

## Critical questions for HCS / RBDR (resolve before pricing)

1. Phase-4 boundary: which specific classrooms (room numbers / SF) per A2.1
2. Cosmetology-station count + shampoo-bowl count (drives 064101 + 224001 + 262726)
3. OFCI vs. CFCI cosmetology stations (digest scope list mentioned "Casework" — clarify whether 064101 is the stations OR the back-of-house cabinetry only, with stations OFCI)
4. Section 012300 Alternates: Alt 1 additive vs. deductive scope; HCS's intent on whether to include Alt 1 in roll-up
5. Section 012100 Allowances: any cash allowances BPC's quote should accommodate (typical: hardware, paint color match, signage)
6. Section 096700 Fluid-Applied Flooring chemistry / thickness / SF — confirm against finish plan A7.1
7. HVAC-exhaust strategy (general classroom vs. cosmetology-specific exhaust hoods over color stations) per MH7.0
8. Schedule: NTP date + substantial-completion date + summer-class blackout dates (MCC summer term overlap risk)
9. Liquidated damages clause language (PM 007200 Gen Conditions or 005213 Agreement Form)
10. Whether HCS requires sub bond on award
11. **Davis-Bacon wage determination** — the McLennan-County, TX, building-construction WD applicable on the contract date (HCS will pull and post in addenda; if not, BPC pulls)
12. Spec §1.01 "netting and artificial turf" template-carry-over — clarification + corrected language
