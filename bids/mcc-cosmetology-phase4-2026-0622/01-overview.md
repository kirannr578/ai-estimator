## Selection criteria (CSP weighted scoring)

Per PM §002116 §1.04, MCC will evaluate proposers on the following weighted factors (BPC tier
is sub-to-HCS, but these criteria affect HCS's MCC submission and therefore HCS's appetite for
particular sub partners):

| Criterion | Weight | BPC implication |
|---|---:|---|
| Proposal price | 25% | HCS is shopping subs to win this lane; price aggressively after DBA wage check |
| Reputation | 10% | HCS values DFWMSDC-channel subs (helps HCS's HUB story) |
| Quality | 10% | BPC's Hindu Temple of Southlake exemplar applies |
| Goods/services meet college needs | 2% | Standard interior reno; minimal differentiation |
| Past relationship with college | 10% | BPC has none with MCC; cannot influence |
| Time of completion + ability to complete on time | 10% | Confirm HCS's schedule plan with HCS Estimating |
| Daily full-time superintendent w/ project-type experience | 10% | BPC supplies its own super only if quoting multi-trade rolled-up package |
| Financial capacity, facilities, equipment, personnel | 8% | BPC's profile supports this on a sub-tier basis |
| **Office location close to college** | **5%** | **Penalty for BPC at 95 mi vs. Waco-domiciled GCs** |
| Knowledge of environmentally-friendly construction | 10% | BPC's restoration / refresh portfolio applies |
