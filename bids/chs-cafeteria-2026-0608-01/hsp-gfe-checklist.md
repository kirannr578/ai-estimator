# CHS Cafeteria Serving Line Renovation — HSP Good-Faith-Effort (GFE) Checklist

> **Use:** Standalone closure checklist for the GFE binder. Required per 34 TAC §20.285. Keep evidence files locally (not in git).

## GFE elements — at least 2 required; do all 5 to be safe

### 1. Scope divided into reasonable HUB-sized portions

- [ ] Per-trade HSP allocation table prepared (see `05-hsp-plan.md` §3)
- [ ] Each trade scoped for HUB capacity (not bundled too large for HUB participation)
- [ ] Specialty / lab / single-source trades flagged as "HUB unavailable" with justification

### 2. Advertised the subcontracting opportunity in ≥ 2 HUB publications

- [ ] Ad placed in TX Comptroller's HUB News: `{{AD_DATE_1}}` — `{{AD_FILE_1}}`
- [ ] Ad placed in `{{HUB_PUBLICATION_2}}` (e.g. Texas Construction News, Greater Houston Black Chamber publication, Hispanic Contractors Association of Texas, Asian American Chamber of Commerce Texas, NAWIC TX): `{{AD_DATE_2}}` — `{{AD_FILE_2}}`
- [ ] Posted on BPC's own subcontractor portal with HUB notice: `{{POSTING_DATE}}` — `{{POSTING_FILE}}`

### 3. Written notice to a representative sample of HUB subcontractors per opportunity

- [ ] Outreach log maintained at `local/hub-outreach-log.csv` (outside git)
- [ ] ≥ 5 HUB subs contacted per trade
- [ ] Each contact logged with: date, trade, sub name, HUB cert #, contact email, method, outcome, bid amount, notes
- [ ] Standardized solicitation packet sent (project name, scope, due date, plans link, certs required, BPC contact)
- [ ] Non-response after ≥ 7 calendar days documented as part of GFE
- [ ] Rejection of HUB sub bid documented with reason (price, schedule, qualification)

### 4. Attended HUB outreach events the agency hosts

- [ ] `Cleburne ISD` HUB outreach event(s) attended: `{{EVENT_DATE_1}}` — `{{EVENT_RECORD_1}}`
- [ ] If no event held in window: documented "no event scheduled during bid window"

### 5. Contacted HUB Discretionary Contracting Forum / agency HUB Operations

- [ ] TTUS: TTUS HUB office contacted: `{{CONTACT_DATE}}` — `{{CONTACT_RECORD}}`
- [ ] UT: UT HUB office contacted: `{{CONTACT_DATE}}` — `{{CONTACT_RECORD}}`
- [ ] State agency: TX Comptroller Statewide HUB program contacted: `{{CONTACT_DATE}}` — `{{CONTACT_RECORD}}`

## GFE binder contents (paper or PDF, organized for evaluator)

1. Cover sheet — project name + solicitation # + signer + date
2. Self-performing justification narrative
3. Per-trade scope-divided table (from §3 of `05-hsp-plan.md`)
4. Advertisement evidence — ads + dates + circulation
5. Per-trade outreach log printout
6. Per-trade sample solicitation packet (one packet, with explanation that all subs received the same)
7. Per-trade response log — bid received / no response / declined / why declined
8. HUB outreach event attendance — sign-in sheets, emails, photos
9. HUB Operations office contact log
10. Final HSP commitment table (from §6 of `05-hsp-plan.md`)

## When commitment ≥ goal

If BPC's HSP commitment meets or exceeds the applicable goal, GFE narrative is briefer but still required. The agency wants to see that BPC followed the procurement process even though the goal was met — demonstrating capability + future-bid discipline.

## When commitment < goal

GFE must be **comprehensive** — the HSP binder is essentially proving why BPC could not meet the goal despite reasonable effort. All 5 GFE elements must be documented with evidence. The agency's HUB office reviews the binder line-by-line.
