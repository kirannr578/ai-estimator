## 4. Assumptions (build into the proposal)

- TX prevailing wage per `Davis-Bacon Prevailing Wage Rates for Johnson County (SAM.gov; attached to RFCSP)` for `Johnson` County per Tex. Gov't Code Ch. 2258
- TX UGSC 2010 + `Cleburne ISD` SGC Article 5 insurance limits apply
- TX UGSC Article 6 — 5% bid bond, 100% P&P on award
- `Cleburne ISD` SGC supplement governs over base UGSC where they conflict
- Building remains: `vacated (summer recess; cafeteria offline during construction)` during construction
- Owner-furnished items: per Section 2 above
- Site access via: `{{SITE_ACCESS_NOTES}}` (badging, escort hours, after-hours window)
- BPC scope ends at: `{{SCOPE_BOUNDARY}}`
- Substantial completion: `49 (NTP 2026-06-16 → 2026-08-03 final completion)` cal days; Final Completion: `49 (mandatory completion by 2026-08-03 per RFCSP §20)` cal days
- LD rate per `Cleburne ISD` SGC: $`500 (per RFCSP §24, $500/calendar day after 2026-08-03)` per calendar day
