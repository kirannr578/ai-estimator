## 5. Notes on agency-specific scoring quirks

- **TAMU System SSC-managed CSPs** often weight past performance and team depth heavily (15–25% each), and price slightly less (40–50%). HSP scored as part of the package, not standalone.
- **TTUS member institutions (TTU, ASU, TTUHSC)** often weight price more heavily (50–60%) and HSP as pass/fail rather than weighted.
- **UT System** varies by member institution; UT Austin Capital Planning weights price + qualifications equally.
- **City + County CSPs** vary widely; weights published in the procurement file.
- **K-12 ISD bond programs** typically weight price 50–70% with the remainder split among technical / past-perf / HSP.
