## 5. Pricing strategy implications

Given the price weight and BPC's tight-markup posture, the pricing strategy in `08-pricing-strategy.md` should:

- Anchor on `265,000` – `325,000` band (refine after sub quotes)
- Apply 5–7% contingency (institutional reno baseline)
- Apply 8–12% overhead
- Apply 5–7% profit
- Stay tight — but not lowest. **Bids > 25% below median often trigger "non-responsibility" risk** at TX state agencies. Aim for competitive, not lowest.

## 6. Schedule strategy implications

Given the schedule weight (typically 5–10%), the schedule posture is:

- **Don't over-promise.** A short SC duration scores marginally higher but exposes BPC to LD risk + execution risk.
- Target SC duration: realistic per [`firm/scope-templates/`](../../../firm/scope-templates/README.md) archetype guidance.
- If schedule weight is < 5%, propose comfortable SC duration with documented sequencing rationale; sacrifice the small schedule-score upside for execution buffer.
- If schedule weight is > 10%, consider proposing aggressive SC duration with documented mitigation (parallel crews, expedited procurement) — but only if the team can deliver.
