## Communication norms

- **Agency PM is the day-to-day single POC.** All routine questions, RFIs, site coordination flow through PM.
- **Contracting Office handles formal contracting actions.** Pricing, contract execution, addenda — through CO.
- **A/E handles design questions** — coordinate via PM where possible (PM expects to see A/E threads).
- **HUB Coordinator** is the post-award HSP-compliance POC. Pre-award HSP questions can go via PM or directly to HUB Coordinator per CSP.
- **Pre-response meeting Q&A** is published — request the published Q&A before pricing.
