## GFE binder contents (paper or PDF, organized for evaluator)

1. Cover sheet — project name + solicitation # + signer + date
2. Self-performing justification narrative
3. Per-trade scope-divided table (from §3 of `05-hsp-plan.md`)
4. Advertisement evidence — ads + dates + circulation
5. Per-trade outreach log printout
6. Per-trade sample solicitation packet (one packet, with explanation that all subs received the same)
7. Per-trade response log — bid received / no response / declined / why declined
8. HUB outreach event attendance — sign-in sheets, emails, photos
9. HUB Operations office contact log
10. Final HSP commitment table (from §6 of `05-hsp-plan.md`)

## When commitment ≥ goal

If BPC's HSP commitment meets or exceeds the applicable goal, GFE narrative is briefer but still required. The agency wants to see that BPC followed the procurement process even though the goal was met — demonstrating capability + future-bid discipline.

## When commitment < goal

GFE must be **comprehensive** — the HSP binder is essentially proving why BPC could not meet the goal despite reasonable effort. All 5 GFE elements must be documented with evidence. The agency's HUB office reviews the binder line-by-line.
