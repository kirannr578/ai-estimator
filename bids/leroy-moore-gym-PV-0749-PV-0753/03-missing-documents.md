## 5. Common RFI categories on TX state CSPs

- **HUB goal interpretation:** is `21.10%` the statewide rule or a project override?
- **HSP deadline:** is HSP due with the proposal or on a separate later deadline?
- **OFCI / OFOI scope flip risk:** confirm which items are owner-supplied (Angelo State Carr EFA had a late lockers OFCI → CFCI flip)
- **Structured cabling responsibility:** often ambiguous; confirm pathway vs cable vs both
- **Lab utility connections:** owner-supplied to roughed-in point or BPC connects?
- **Sub-list-at-proposal vs at-award:** some CSPs require sub list at proposal; some defer
- **CSA flow-down (especially Master Vendor Agreement terms if SSC-managed):** read non-solicit + non-compete clauses + Net-75 payment vs Net-30 before pricing
- **LD rate confirmation:** verify $/cal day
- **Pre-response meeting Q&A:** request published Q&A copy
