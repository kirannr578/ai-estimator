## 3. Scope summary

Targeted repairs + maintenance at the San Juan Mission Restroom (LCS #22996) — a **nationally significant historic structure** within the San Antonio Missions NHP. The restroom is ~400 SF covering men's and women's sections.

**Specific work includes:**
- **Electrical** — heater/exhaust/light units, panel, LED lighting, fans, emergency lighting
- **Plumbing + fixtures** — ADA sinks, touchless faucets, mirrors
- **Cabinetry + storage** — new cedar cabinets/shelves
- **Interior repairs + preservation** — partition walls, tile/grout cleaning, ceiling repairs, painting
- **Temporary facilities** — portable ADA-compliant toilets during construction

## 4. Specialty considerations (CRITICAL for BPC)

⚠️ **HISTORIC PRESERVATION** is the central scope constraint:

- LCS #22996 = Listed on the National Register of Historic Places (LCS = List of Classified Structures)
- All work must comply with **Secretary of the Interior's Standards for Rehabilitation** (36 CFR 67)
- All work must be reviewed by an NPS historic preservation officer (HPO)
- Materials must match historic character (e.g., cedar cabinetry, not modern composite)
- Methods must be **non-invasive** to historic fabric

⚠️ **BPC's past-performance gap:** Per `firm/firm-profile.json`, BPC has **no documented historic preservation work**. The closest comparable past performance:
- Hindu Temple of Southlake — religious institutional, NOT historic-registered
- Lavon RV Park — new construction, NOT preservation
- Holiday Inn (Hall Park) — commercial reno, NOT preservation

**This is a real go/no-go consideration when the solicitation drops.** BPC may not score well on past-performance for true historic preservation experience.
