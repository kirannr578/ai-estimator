## What's NOT in scope (informational)

- ❌ **Structural deck repair** — PDF specifies "into the metal deck" / fully-adhered upper deck but does not include rotted-deck or damaged-substrate repair scope. **BPC should call out a deck-repair allowance** (e.g., $X/SF for any deck found compromised during tear-off, additive change-order basis)
- ❌ **Insulation upgrade** — PDF specifies only the 1/2" HD ISO **cover board**. Existing insulation thickness/condition not in scope. If owner ever asks for re-insulation, that's a separate scope.
- ❌ **Lightning protection** — not mentioned. If existing lightning protection is on the roof, BPC needs to coordinate with a specialty sub for take-down + re-install (not BPC self-perform).
- ❌ **Roof equipment work** — HVAC condensers, exhaust fans, antennas, etc. on the roof. PDF doesn't mention; if present, BPC's tear-off must coordinate with TMD's MEP shop for temporary disconnect / re-mount.
- ❌ **Hazmat abatement** — PDF doesn't mention, but older built-up roofs (which the gravel reference implies) can have asphalt mastic or older mod-bit layers with low-level hazmat. **BPC's quote should call out inclusion / exclusion explicitly.**
- ❌ **Interior repairs** — water-damage repair below leaking sections of existing roof is not in scope. Owner-directed repair to interior ceiling tiles is a separate change order.
