## Headline risks

- ⚠️ **Davis-Bacon / TX Ch. 2258 prevailing wages apply.** PDF §5.2 explicitly cites *"wage scale of various classes of labor as set forth in 29 USC Section 206. Published wage rates for the State of Texas and county of the project can be located at https://beta.sam.gov/ (Davis-Bacon Act) pursuant to Chapter 2258, Texas Government Code"*. Adds material labor-cost burden on BPC's self-perform crew + WH-347-style certified payroll. Pull the **Lamar County, TX, building-construction wage determination** from sam.gov before pricing.
- ⚠️ **Buy American Act applies.** PDF §1.5 explicitly invokes 41 U.S.C. 10. All materials (TPO membrane, HD ISO board, plywood, drip edge, CF, gutters/downspouts) must be Buy-American-Act-compliant. Confirm with TPO membrane manufacturer (Carlisle, Firestone/HoltFlex, GAF, Sika Sarnafil) on country-of-origin attestation.
- ⚠️ **Background checks required by Owner (TMD).** PDF §5.1: Sub and Sub's employees must submit to background check conducted by the Owner. Process + lead time + escort posture for Camp Maxey access are not described — confirm at site visit. National Guard installations sometimes require REAL ID compliance / driver's license + criminal-history attestation.
- ⚠️ **Photos attachment is missing from the drop.** PDF §3.A references *"Photos, Camp Maxey Roof Replacement.pdf"* but page 7 is just the heading "PHOTOS:" with no image content; companion file is not in the drop. **Request from Patrick Grabowski** before pricing — photos likely show existing-roof condition + current drip edge / counterflashing details that drive demo + replacement quantities.
- ⚠️ **Roof SF not stated** — PDF gives the system spec but no roof area dimensions. Without SF, BPC cannot accurately price. **The site visit (6/1) is the gate** to obtain measurements (PDF §2.2: "Subcontractor to verify all measurements") + roof access conditions.
- ⚠️ **Insurance status (COI expired per profile).** BPC's CGL SBCC-042443-00 expired 2024-09-25 per `firm/firm-profile.md` §7. ATS will not contract uninsured. Renewal in flight; needed COI naming Adam's Trade & Services, Inc. as additional insured (PDF §12.D) per the explicit AI requirement.
