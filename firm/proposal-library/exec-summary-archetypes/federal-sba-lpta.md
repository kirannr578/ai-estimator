## Notes on what to KEEP OUT of this cover letter

LPTA price proposals are scored on price + Pass/Fail technical acceptability. The cover letter should not include any of the following — each is an opportunity to inadvertently introduce an exception that flips the bid Pass → Fail:

- **"Why us" pitch** — not scored on LPTA
- **Past performance summaries** — those go in Volume II
- **Schedule narrative** — submittal stage, post-award
- **Safety / QC plan teaser** — submittal stage, post-award
- **Exceptions, clarifications, qualifications** — these are kiss-of-death; raise as RFI **before** the cutoff, not in the cover letter
- **Alternates not requested in the RFP** — non-responsive
- **Anything about pricing methodology** — the CLINs are the price; methodology stays internal
