### Role on this project

As QA/QC Lead, `[USER TO FILL — First name]` implements the **3-Phase QC Plan** (per [`firm/proposal-library/boilerplate/qa-qc-plan-one-pager.md`](../boilerplate/qa-qc-plan-one-pager.md)):

- **Preparatory Phase** — held before each major scope item begins. Reviews submittals, drawings, specifications, AHAs, materials, and crew readiness. Documented on a Preparatory Phase Inspection form.
- **Initial Phase** — held after the first ~10% of each scope item is complete. Verifies workmanship matches specification + approved submittals. Documents acceptance criteria for the crew.
- **Follow-Up Phase** — daily during the scope item. Spot-check inspections, recorded in the daily QC report.
- **Completion / Closeout** — final inspection at substantial completion against the punch list.

The QA/QC Lead also:

- Maintains the **QC log book** on site (submittal status, inspection records, NCR — non-conformance reports, deficiency closure tracking)
- Coordinates **third-party special inspections** (e.g. structural welding, soils, concrete) as called for by IBC Ch. 17
- Approves all **mock-ups** before scope item execution (paint mock-up, flooring mock-up, tile mock-up)
- Verifies all **material submittals** match approved cuts before installation
- Conducts the **pre-final + final walk** with owner and the AHJ
- Reports to the Project Manager + Project Executive weekly
