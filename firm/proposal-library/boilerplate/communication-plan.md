## 2. Meeting cadence

| Meeting | Cadence | Attendees | Purpose | Minutes by |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Project kickoff | Within 5 working days of NTP | BPC PM + Super + Safety + QC + Sub foremen; `{{AGENCY}}` PM + A/E + EHS + facility owner-rep | Confirm scope, schedule, contacts, site logistics, communication protocols, submittals, safety | BPC PM |
| Owner-Architect-Contractor (OAC) meeting | Weekly, same day + time | BPC PM + Super; `{{AGENCY}}` PM + A/E rep | Schedule update, RFI status, submittal status, change-order status, open items, look-ahead | BPC PM (published within 24 hrs) |
| Sub-foreman coordination | Weekly, day before OAC | BPC PM + Super + each active sub foreman | Resolve trade-stacking, sequence, materials lead, RFIs to escalate | BPC Super |
| Pre-installation conference (per scope item) | 1–2 days before each major scope starts | BPC Super + QC + crew + sub foreman | Preparatory Phase Inspection per QC plan | BPC QC Lead |
| Daily tailgate / safety brief | Daily 7:00 AM | All on-site crew | Day's work plan + AHAs + hazards | BPC Super |
| Toolbox talks | Weekly, 15 min | All on-site crew | OSHA topic rotation | Safety Lead |
| Look-ahead schedule | 3-week look-ahead, published weekly | — | Out next 3 weeks of crew + sub + delivery + inspection activity | BPC PM |
| Monthly progress meeting + pay-app review | Monthly, mid-month | BPC PM + Principal; `{{AGENCY}}` PM + Owner-rep | Monthly progress + pay app review + cost-to-complete + open items | BPC PM |

## 3. Communication channels

| Channel | Use |
|---|---|
| **Email** | Formal correspondence — RFIs, RFI responses, submittals, change-order requests + responses, meeting minutes, schedule updates |
| **`{{PROJECT_PORTAL}}`** (e-Builder, Procore, ProjectMates, SharePoint per `{{AGENCY}}` standard) | Document control — drawings, submittals, RFIs, change orders, pay apps, schedule, daily reports |
| **Text / phone** | Urgent field issues (active hazard, on-site question requiring same-day answer, after-hours owner notification) |
| **OAC meeting** | Substantive scope, schedule, cost conversations |
| **Daily report** | Routine field information |

## 4. Response-time commitments

| Item | BPC commitment |
|---|---|
| RFI from `{{AGENCY}}` / A/E to BPC requiring same-day field decision | Acknowledged within 2 hours; responded within 8 hours |
| Routine RFI to A/E | Issued by BPC within 5 working days of identification; tracked on RFI log; A/E response expected within 10 working days per spec |
| Submittal review by BPC | Within 5 working days of receipt from sub |
| Change-order proposal (cost + schedule impact) | Within 10 working days of change directive |
| Daily Construction Report | Filed by EOB each work day |
| Weekly look-ahead | Published every Monday by 5:00 PM |
| Pay application | Submitted by 5th working day of each month |
| Lien waivers (conditional + unconditional) | Submitted with each pay app |

## 5. Document control

All formal project documents (RFIs, submittals, change orders, pay apps, drawings, specifications, meeting minutes, inspection reports, NCRs, daily reports) are stored in **`{{PROJECT_PORTAL}}`** with consistent naming convention:

- `[YYYY-MM-DD] [DOC-TYPE]_[NUMBER]_[SHORT-NAME].pdf`
  - Example: `2026-08-12 RFI_003_Ceiling-Grid-Conflict.pdf`
  - Example: `2026-08-15 SUBMITTAL_09-65-19_LVT-Color-Samples.pdf`

BPC also maintains a parallel local archive of all project documents through closeout.
