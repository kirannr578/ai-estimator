## Paste-ready narrative (1–2 paragraphs)

Blue Print Constructs is the general contractor on the Hindu Temple of Southlake renovation — a ~10,700 SF place-of-worship renovation in Southlake, TX, for the North Texas Hindu Heritage Society. The project covers interior finish-out (drywall, paint, flooring, ceilings), restroom partitions and accessories, MEP scope coordination, and architectural / structural compliance under Tarrant County jurisdiction (Architecture sheets dated 09-12-2025; Structural sheets dated 09-12-2025).

The project demonstrates BPC's ability to deliver a high-finish institutional renovation on a negotiated-lump-sum, owner-direct basis with a faith-based owner — managing trade sequencing, material procurement (phenolic toilet partitions and similar specialty items), and coordination with the owner's design team and county AHJ. The renovation is in active execution and is on track to meet the owner's substantial-completion target.
