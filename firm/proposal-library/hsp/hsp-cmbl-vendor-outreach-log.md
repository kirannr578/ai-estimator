# HSP — CMBL HUB Vendor Outreach Log

> **How to use this template** — Per-vendor outreach log; the underlying evidence for the GFE narrative ([`hsp-good-faith-effort.md`](hsp-good-faith-effort.md)) and required by 34 TAC §20.285(d)(2)–(d)(4). Populate **one row per HUB vendor contact per solicitation per scope**. Source the candidate list from the TX Comptroller CMBL HUB Search at <https://comptroller.texas.gov/purchasing/vendor/hub/search.php>; supplement with agency-provided HUB vendor lists. Target ≥ 3 contacts per trade (≥ 5 preferred). Keep the underlying email correspondence archived alongside this log. Search-and-replace every `{{PLACEHOLDER}}`.

## 4. Outreach methods used

For each contact above, the contact method falls in one of these categories:

- **Email** — primary method; solicitation packet attached, return receipt requested
- **Phone** — used when vendor has not responded to email after `{{PHONE_FOLLOWUP_DAYS}}` working days
- **Mail** — courier or USPS Certified Mail with return receipt, used when email and phone do not yield a response
- **Portal** — agency-specific HUB portal where the agency facilitates outreach (e.g. TAMU System SSC HUB portal)
- **In-person** — at a HUB outreach event the agency or BPC hosted

Email archive evidence stored at `{{EMAIL_ARCHIVE_REF}}`. Phone-call records (date / time / minutes) at `{{PHONE_LOG_REF}}`. Mail receipts at `{{MAIL_RECEIPT_REF}}`.

## 5. Non-response handling

For each HUB vendor that did not respond:

- **Followed up at least once** by email at `{{FOLLOWUP_DAYS}}` working days after initial contact
- **Followed up at least once** by phone at `{{FOLLOWUP_PHONE_DAYS}}` working days after initial contact
- **Documented as non-responsive** in the log above with a short reason if available (e.g. "voicemail full", "phone disconnected", "busy season — no capacity per voicemail").

> **Discipline reminder** — a non-responsive HUB vendor is acceptable evidence of GFE only when BPC has documented at least two contact attempts via at least two channels.
